About Boli

Boli is an innovative sign language translation system that bridges the communication gap between the hearing impaired and the hearing world. Our mission is to make communication more accessible and inclusive for everyone.

Our Technology

Boli uses advanced computer vision and machine learning technologies to recognize and interpret sign language gestures in real-time. The system can detect both single-hand and two-hand gestures, making it more comprehensive than traditional solutions.

Features

  • Real-time gesture recognition
  • Support for 30+ common sign language gestures
  • Two-hand gesture detection
  • Text-to-speech conversion
  • User-friendly interface
  • Comprehensive gesture library

Our Vision

We envision a world where communication barriers are eliminated, and everyone can express themselves freely. Boli is just the beginning of our journey towards creating more inclusive technology solutions.
