About BOLI

Breaking communication barriers through innovative sign language translation technology

Our Mission

BOLI is dedicated to creating an inclusive world where communication barriers are eliminated. Our mission is to empower the hearing-impaired community by providing real-time, accurate sign language translation technology that bridges the gap between sign language and spoken language.

Key Features

  • Real-time Translation

    Instant translation of sign language gestures into spoken language with minimal latency.

  • Comprehensive Gesture Library

    Support for 30+ common sign language gestures with high accuracy recognition.

  • Two-Hand Detection

    Advanced technology to recognize and interpret complex two-hand gestures.

  • Audio Feedback

    Real-time audio output to provide immediate feedback on recognized gestures.

Our Technology

BOLI leverages cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning technologies to provide accurate sign language translation. Our system uses MediaPipe for hand tracking and custom gesture recognition algorithms to identify and interpret sign language gestures in real-time.
